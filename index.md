## About the Workshop:

The 1st edition of the PRiSM workshop will join forces with the 20th anniversary of the Augmented Environments for Computer-Assisted Interventions ([AE-CAI](https://workshops.ap-lab.ca/aecai2026/)), Computer-Assisted Robotic Endoscopy ([CARE](https://workshops.ap-lab.ca/aecai2026/)), and Context Aware Operating Theaters ([OR 2.0](https://workshops.ap-lab.ca/aecai2026/)) workshops. Together, we form a premier co-located full-day event hosted @ MICCAI in Strasbourg, France.

From planning and intraoperative guidance to clinical training, deployment, and translation, this joint platform brings together researchers, clinicians, and industry partners. While PRiSM continues its strong track on mixed-reality-enabled intervention and clinically grounded imaging-to-intervention workflows, our combined workshop now broadly covers advanced endoscopy, robotic interventions, and intelligent operating theaters.

## Why Join Us?

- Dedicated focus on complete CAI workflows, from mixed reality to surgical robotics and smart operating rooms
- Strong emphasis on clinical translation and deployment
- **Call for Demos:** Hands-on demo experiences showcasing intervention systems and translational prototypes. Accepted demos will be showcased on Sept 27th and featured in the special issue proceedings!
- Cross-disciplinary participation from academia, clinics, and industry
- **Special Issue Proceedings:** Workshop proceedings will be published in a Special Issue of Wiley's IET Healthcare Technology Letters (HTL) open access journal, with a 50% discount and potential waivers.

## Topics of Interest

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

- Augmented, virtual and mixed reality Medical image visualization and understanding
- Surgical simulation and training
- Image-guided and robotic interventions
- HCI considerations in computer-assisted interventions
- Accessible and affordable surgical technologies
- Multi-modal registration and Fusion
- Tool/tissue tracking and calibration
- Digital twins for computer-assisted interventions
- Integrated Computer-assisted Intervention platforms
- Tele-presence and remote care/delivery/interventions

*For topics specifically related to CARE and OR 2.0, please visit our [Joint Event Website](https://workshops.ap-lab.ca/aecai2026/).*

In addition to the above, we explicitly welcome medical imaging and intervention papers with clear CAI relevance and clinical translation value.
